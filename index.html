History

From a hackathon momentum to a quantum community.

The idea behind Quantum Research Technologies was born from the energy of the Quantum Computing Hackathon, where students, researchers, and industry actors worked around concrete quantum challenges. After this experience with the City of London Corporation and IBM, the three co-founders initiated QRT to keep that momentum alive, turning a one-time event into a long-term association where academic talent, industrial needs, and entrepreneurial ambition can meet.
