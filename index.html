Scientific orientation

Connecting physics training to concrete industrial problems

The Lebanese Physics Society aims to gather Lebanese students, researchers, engineers, and professionals around physics-based technologies, with a special focus on quantum science, photonics, artificial intelligence, modelling, sensing, and industrial innovation.

Quantum applications

Quantum branches and use cases

A first map of the fields we want to connect inside the society.

Quantum for finance

Optimization, risk and market modelling

Quantum algorithms and quantum-inspired methods for portfolio optimization, Monte Carlo acceleration, risk analysis, derivative pricing, and complex financial systems.

Open the French Qubits hackathon example →
Quantum for modelling

Simulation of complex physical systems

Quantum simulation and numerical modelling for materials, chemistry, condensed matter, wave propagation, differential equations, and high-dimensional physical problems.

Quantum & AI

Learning, control and physical AI

Connections between quantum physics and artificial intelligence, including quantum machine learning, AI-assisted experiments, photonic AI, inverse design, and control of quantum systems.

Quantum sensing

Metrology and environmental monitoring

Quantum and photonic sensors for time-frequency transfer, optical fiber sensing, geophysics, seismic monitoring, navigation, biomedical sensing, and industrial diagnostics.

Photonics

Integrated and industrial photonic systems

Photonic integrated circuits, lithium niobate platforms, semiconductor lasers, nonlinear optics, telecom components, and optical systems for real-world deployment.

Quantum networks

Communication and secure infrastructure

Quantum communication, secure optical links, synchronization networks, quantum internet architectures, and the interface between telecom infrastructure and quantum technologies.

For students and young researchers

Build a Lebanese network across universities, laboratories, and companies, share opportunities, find mentors, and discover industrial paths for physics profiles.

For industry and innovation

Promote physics-based expertise in quantum technologies, AI, photonics, modelling, sensing, and data-driven scientific engineering.
